About The Firm

A chambers built on precedent, not promises

Founded in 1994 by a small group of barristers returning from practice abroad, The Lawyers and Jurists has grown into one of Dhaka’s established full-service firms, retaining the same emphasis on rigorous preparation and direct partner involvement that defined its earliest matters.

Today the firm advises listed companies, foreign missions, financial institutions and private clients, while maintaining a pro bono docket that reflects the founders’ original commitment to access to justice.
