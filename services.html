Our Services

Eighteen practice areas, each led by a partner, covering the matters that most commonly confront individuals, enterprises and institutions.

§ 01

Corporate & Commercial Law

Company formation, governance, shareholder agreements, mergers and joint ventures for domestic and foreign enterprises.

§ 02

Litigation & Dispute Resolution

Representation before the Supreme Court, District Courts and specialised tribunals in civil and commercial disputes.

§ 03

Banking & Finance

Syndicated lending, security documentation, loan restructuring and regulatory compliance for financial institutions.

§ 04

Intellectual Property

Trademark, patent and copyright registration, portfolio management and infringement enforcement.

§ 05

Real Estate & Property Law

Title due diligence, RAJUK approvals, commercial leasing and development agreements.

§ 06

Labour & Employment

Workplace policy, termination disputes, factory compliance and collective bargaining representation.

§ 07

Family & Matrimonial Law

Divorce, custody, maintenance and inheritance matters, with an emphasis on mediated resolution.

§ 08

Tax & Regulatory Advisory

Corporate tax planning, transfer pricing, customs matters and NBR compliance.

§ 09

Criminal Defence

Representation in criminal proceedings before Magistrate, Sessions and Special Tribunal courts.

§ 10

Constitutional & Public Interest Law

Writ petitions, judicial review and public interest litigation before the High Court Division.

§ 11

Maritime & Shipping Law

Cargo claims, charter-party disputes and port authority matters for shipping and logistics clients.

§ 12

Arbitration & Mediation (ADR)

Domestic and international arbitration, institutional mediation and structured settlement negotiation.

§ 13

Immigration & Foreign Investment

Work permits, visa sponsorship and regulatory approval for foreign direct investment.

§ 14

Insurance Law

Policy drafting, claims disputes and regulatory advisory for insurers and policyholders.

§ 15

Environmental Law

Compliance advisory and dispute representation on environmental clearance and industrial regulation.

§ 16

Media & Telecommunications

Licensing, content regulation and commercial agreements for broadcast and telecom operators.

§ 17

Education & Non-Profit Law

Governance structuring and regulatory compliance for educational institutions and NGOs.

§ 18

Debt Recovery & Insolvency

Recovery proceedings under the Artha Rin Adalat Ain and insolvency advisory for creditors and debtors.
