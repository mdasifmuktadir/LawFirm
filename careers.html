Why Join Us

Direct partner mentorship from day one

Associates at The Lawyers and Jurists are staffed directly under a partner rather than routed through multiple layers of seniority, giving early exposure to client meetings, hearings and negotiation.

The firm supports Bar Council enrolment, sponsors continuing legal education, and maintains a structured rotation for junior associates across at least two practice groups in their first three years.
