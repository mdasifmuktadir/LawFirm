About The Firm

Three decades of chambers practice, grounded in preparation, precedent and direct partner involvement.

Our Story

Founded on a simple premise

The Lawyers and Jurists was established in 1994 by four barristers who believed that clients were better served by a chambers that combined common-law training with a close, practical understanding of Bangladesh’s courts and regulatory institutions.

What began as a five-member litigation practice in Motijheel has since grown into a full-service firm of over forty advocates and counsel, spanning eighteen practice areas, while retaining the founders’ original insistence that a partner — not a junior associate alone — carries responsibility for every matter.

The firm relocated to its current chambers in Dhanmondi in 2011, where it maintains a dedicated hearing-preparation library and a moot room used for internal advocacy training.

Founding Partners, 1994
Mission

Counsel that withstands scrutiny

To provide legal advice that is rigorously prepared, plainly communicated, and able to withstand the closest scrutiny of a court, regulator or counterparty.

Vision

A reference chambers for the region

To be recognised across South Asia as the chambers of first call for complex, high-stakes commercial and constitutional matters.

Approach

Partner-led, evidence-first

Every engagement is led by a partner and grounded in documentary evidence before strategy is proposed to the client.

What We Stand For

Our core values

I

Integrity

We advise clients honestly on the merits of a matter, including when the answer is not the one they hoped to hear.

II

Diligence

Every brief is prepared as though it will be tested by the most exacting opposing counsel in the room.

III

Confidentiality

Client information is held in strict confidence, governed by internal protocols reviewed annually.

IV
